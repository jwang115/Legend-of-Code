Question: What are Variables?

Answer: You could thing of them as storage units for values. The three major types of variables are: int, float, and string (words). Instead of having to repeatedly retrieve the same data, the variable is stored in computer memory once and used wherever needed.

To initialize a variable, write var varName = value. The varName and value can be whatever you want them to be.
